Our Story

A legacy of taste and hospitality

Crafting Culinary Excellence

Invas Multicuisine Restaurant was born out of a passion for authentic flavors and a desire to create a space where families and friends can come together for more than just a meal.

Located in the bustling Samatha Nagar area of Gajuwaka, we take pride in serving a diverse range of dishes, from traditional Andhra delicacies to sophisticated North Indian curries and mouth-watering seafood.

Our commitment to quality ingredients and professional service has made us a favorite destination for food lovers in Vizag.
